Strawberry Smoothie

Description

Chilled, health, refreshing drink.

Ingredients

  • 50 grams peanuts
  • 250 ml sparkling water
  • A cup of fresh strawberies
  • Two table spoons of raw honey
  • Ice-cubes

Steps

  1. Put the peanuts, sparkiling water, strawberries and raw honey in a blender
  2. Blend till hardly any solid particles are visible
  3. Pour the content from the blender into a cup through a strain
  4. Add ice-cube and enjoy
