Cheese Salad

Description

Fresh, samptuous, mouth watering meal straight from the lake to your plate. Ideal for casual dinning or weekend backyard parties.

Ingredients

  • A cup of grated Mozzarrela cheese
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Lettuce
  • Broccoli
  • Two boiled eggs

Steps

  1. Chop the lettuce and the broccoli together and set on a plate
  2. Add the mushrooms and mix together
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Break the boiled eggs and add to the miture
  5. Sprinkle olive oil
  6. Serve
