Grilled Tilapia

Description

Fresh, samptuous, mouth watering meal straight from the lake to your plate. Ideal for casual dinning or weekend backyard parties.

Ingredients

  • One mid-sized tilapia fish
  • 100g molten butter
  • 35ml soy sauce
  • 25g Lucile mixed herbs
  • Seasoning (Salt & Pepper)
  • 5 cloves of garlic; ground

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the grill to around 150ËšC
  2. Make seven slits on the fish diagonnaly
  3. Apply molten butter generously, followed by Lucile's mixed herbs and seasoning
  4. Gently place the fish on the grill and wait 15 minutes before turning the other side
  5. Brush with soy sauce continuously for 2 minutes on both sides
  6. Remove when slightly browned and sprinkle ground garlic while still sizzling hot
  7. Enjoy while still hot
